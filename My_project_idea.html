My Project Idea

I have an idea; it’s an app for men to connect with other men who are at a similar stage in life, whether they’re struggling with their mental health, it’s the first time being a father, or they just want some guidance, I'm also considering the idea of similar interests, to maybe encourage men to seek friendship when times are tough. I wish to create an app and an online platform that would be able to better support men, by giving them access to people who may also be at the same stage in life or wanting to hang out.

The idea stems from an app I saw the other day called “Peanut-app”, which claims to “provide a safe place for women to talk and get support”, which I think is an excellent idea, but it is only for women. Therefore, my concept bloomed; I aim to provide men with a safe place to find support. My motivation would be myself; I was medically discharged from the army for mental health, and when I needed help, I realised that men specifically don’t have much support. We have the regular support networks, although being in the army, not everyone understands and can help, so I aim to implement a section within the app for ex-serving defence members to find others who have been through the same issue or are going through it and seeking support.
